How to Start an Online Shop

Starting your own online store takes just a few steps.
1. First you'll choose a unique domain name.
2. Then you'll pick a template and customize your design to match your brands colors and fonts.
3. Next, fill out your store settings.
4. Add your product photos and descriptions, and you're ready to start taking orders.
