Our leaders

When you’re driving success for thousands of merchants, it’s vital to have a steady hand on the wheel. Our executives have experience growing companies like PayPal, HomeAway and Twitter. They’re supported by a board of distinguished business leaders who know that building a great company takes unfailing commitment to your customers.


Experienced executives

Brent Bellm
CEO

Brent is the CEO of BigCommerce. A retail and ecommerce veteran of 20 years, Brent has served in a variety of executive leadership positions, successfully growing industry-leading companies including HomeAway and PayPal.

Robert Alvarez
CFO & COO

Robert is the Chief Financial Officer and the Chief Operations Officer at BigCommerce. He has been instrumental in the acquisition or listing of his 4 previous companies and brings extensive experience in finance, planning, and business transactions.

Jimmy Duvall
Chief Product Officer

Jimmy is Chief Product Officer for BigCommerce. Prior to BigCommerce he served in key product leadership roles at Hootsuite, Magento, GSI Commerce and Yahoo!

Brian Dhatt
Chief Technology Officer

Brian is the Chief Technology Officer for BigCommerce. Prior to BigCommerce, he served as CTO at Borderfree, where he led all aspects of product and technology, and previously held senior leadership positions at Jetsetter, Gilt City and Best Buy.

Alexandra Shapiro
Chief Marketing Officer

Alexandra is the Chief Marketing Officer for BigCommerce. She has over 15 years of experience driving profitable growth and revenue creation for both B2B and B2C companies, including PayPal, WaMu/Providian and McKinsey & Company.
