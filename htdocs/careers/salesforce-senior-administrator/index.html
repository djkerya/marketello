Salesforce Senior Administrator (6 Month Contract)

Austin Four Points Office

Senior Salesforce Administrator

Summary:

BigCommerce, named a "Best Place to Work" in Austin 2016 is looking for a driven, Senior Salesforce Administrator to join our Business Applications team to ensure that core business processes are supported, maintained, and constantly improving. As a key member of our team you will work with our Salesforce Administrators, Product Engineers, and Key Stakeholders to develop solutions to support our ever evolving Business.  

 Duties and responsibilities

  • Assist with all administrative functions associated with our Salesforce.com CRM  
  • User administration
  • Maintain security role hierarchy and related structures
  • Maintain security profiles and access rights for users within the application
  • Maintain, add, or delete system rules, such as auto-sharing and lead assignments
  • Configure custom reports and dashboards, standard UI options (page layouts, list views) for groups of users
  • Configure Standard and Custom Objects (e.g. Fields, Picklists, Validation Rules, Workflows)
  • Provide routine reports and status updates to management
  • Troubleshoot Production Issues
  • Ad-hoc data Loads
  • Install and Configure Salesforce Exchange Apps
  • Partner with sales operations and senior leadership to understand system challenges

Who You Are:

  • Bachelor’s degree in technical discipline or equivalent professional experience.
  • 7+ years’ experience in Salesforce.
  • 3+ years hands on experience managing large Salesforce Systems, 500+ Users.
  • Experience with one of the following: Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Communities, and Knowledge.
  • Ability to take ownership of projects and be self-directed.
  • Ability to estimate tasks accurately and meet agreed upon deadlines.
  • Comfortable with ambiguity and ability to prioritize in a rapidly changing environment.
  • Clear and effective written and verbal communication skills.
  • Strong technical aptitude and ability to research & solve complex issues independently
  • Possess proven ability to multi-task various duties and assignments while maintaining quality work

Nice to have:

  • Salesforce Certifications.
  • Experience with eCommerce.
  • Experience with Fraud detection/prevention.
  • Experience with other CRMs.
  • Experience with Salesforce Sales Cloud and Service Cloud.
