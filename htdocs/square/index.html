The best way to sell, online and off

For merchants in the US, Canada, and Australia, BigCommerce features a native integration with Square POS, the platform trusted by more than 2 million merchants. Get the ultimate solution for selling online and in person, with a beautiful ecommerce store that’s seamlessly connected to your brick-and-mortar operation.



More ways to pay

Square lets you accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, Android Pay, Apple Watch, debit cards, gift cards and prepaid cards.

Fast payouts

Square deposits funds from your sales the next business day, versus waiting 2–6 business days like many other POS solutions.

Quick, easy setup

Set up the Square POS system in minutes, then connect to BigCommerce to sync your online inventory or quickly open a new online store.

The Square POS inventory sync and catalog import are exactly what I was looking for. Other platforms take up to 24 hours to update inventory, but BigCommerce captures changes instantly.Alexander Head, Drink Dispatch

Connect Square and BigCommerce to streamline your business

Integrating the Square POS with BigCommerce gives you a better way to run your business. You’ll use Square to process all your payments — both online and off — plus get a single location to process and track orders.



Seamlessly manage your inventory

Our two-way sync with Square POS saves you time so you can focus on your business. Inventory is instantly updated when you make a sale, eliminating manual updates, data entry errors and overselling.

See all your sales at a glance

Square Dashboard offers a single view of all your orders, both those you take in person and those made online. Using the Square Dashboard app, you can keep track of real-time sales data on the go.

We had struggled in the past to keep track of items that were available both online and in store, and occasionally had mishaps trying to maintain accurate counts. Now, it's not an issue!Jessica Tata, Son of a Sailor

Offer in-person payment or local delivery

With BigCommerce and Square, you can give customers more flexibility in how they buy and receive your products. Seamlessly offer the ability to shop online and pay in store, or buy online and have their orders delivered.

Reduce costs with competitive, transparent pricing

Unlike other ecommerce POS solutions, BigCommerce doesn’t charge a monthly fee to use Square or require costly third-party apps. Our Square integration is free to use, with inventory syncing included at no extra cost. You have access to Square’s fair, transparent pricing, so you only pay one processing fee per transaction — there are never any surprises.
