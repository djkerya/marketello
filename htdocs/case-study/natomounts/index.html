NatoMounts goes all-in on mobile to achieve 5% mobile conversion rates

How one entrepreneur leveraged digital wallets to grow his portfolio of online stores

Growth by testing and optimization


Brandon Chatham runs around 30 various stores on BigCommerce, but his tech accessory ecommerce store, NatoMounts, is unique.

Around 90% percent of NatoMounts’ traffic comes from mobile devices and nearly 85% of his sales are on mobile. In fact, when launching his website and testing his mobile optimization, he was able to get a new visitor on the site and checked-out in 43 seconds.

The results are the outcome of hyper-focus on mobile and various A/B tests in combination with Chatham’s use of BigCommerce’s one-touch payment integrations with PayPal, Amazon and Apple

BigCommerce has helped me process more than $20 million over the last six years.Brandon Chatham, Founder & CEO of NatoMounts

More than $20 million processed through BigCommerce

As told to BigCommerce by Brandon Chatham, Founder & CEO of NatoMounts.

BigCommerce has helped me process more than $20 million over the last six years.

I have 30 BigCommerce sites up and the reason I keep coming back is because my development staff and design staff are familiar with the templates and the backend. We can quickly create a website or create a duplicate website for A/B testing in less than a few days.

By building these integrations with companies like PayPal and Amazon, BigCommerce provides non-household brands, like me, instant credibility. This equals a clear path to ~5% conversion rates on mobile with over 80% of sales on mobile.

Native integrations with mobile wallets grow revenue

PayPal One-Touch, Amazon Pay, and Apple Pay lead to a 5% mobile conversion rate.

As told to BigCommerce by Brandon Chatham, Founder & CEO of NatoMounts.

BigCommerce has provided the out-of-the-box template, which has worked for tens of thousands of transactions. PayPal makes up 50%, credit cards with Stripe come in at 25%, Amazon Pay makes up about 20%, and Apple Pay makes up the rest.

By building these integrations with companies like PayPal and Amazon, BigCommerce provides non-household brands, like me, instant credibility. This equals a clear path to ~5% conversion rates on mobile with over 80% of sales on mobile.


Using ShipStation to optimize for same-day shipping
