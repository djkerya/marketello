What people do not say – rather what you do not get given you are talking to unskilled people is just how hard it can be to perform what appear to be simple graphic design tasks.

Clipping Path Services and Process https://goo.gl/gBxm8z
Above we discussed DIY tools, the problem is not the tools but the designer manipulating the tool.
Clipping is a delicate activity that involves shearing a section of an image for use elsewhere. When done poorly anyone can spot the difference from miles away. This is because the image you are manipulating is a geometric object. Each photo is in one way or another affected by the background as you look at it in its original form – hence the need for accuracy.
This picture illustrates the primary reason we offer clipping path services to our clients.
While this could be one of if not your best photo in years, just clipping the person out of the photo is an arduous task.
The biggest challenge in this case is the hair. If you took a very high quality image it will have plenty of pixel data that you do not want to lose. Just getting the above image will require serious graphic design skill and several layers using very advanced design software. You will most likely require a mask to capture the face and hair on separate layers. You may need to use the blur tool to make the sections lost less obvious.
This image of the lovely lady in a bikini is another classic example where both background removal and clipping path services are required.
A closer look will reveal that the job is not quite done yet – there is hologram like text still splattered across the image. Nevertheless, the clipping has been done perfectly leaving all the copies of the lady and bikini (subject of the photo) intact.
