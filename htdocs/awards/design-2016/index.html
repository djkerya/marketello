BIGCOMMERCE

DESIGN AWARDS

CELEBRATING EXCELLENCE
IN ECOMMERCE DESIGN

The BigCommerce Design Awards recognize the industry’s most beautiful online storefronts. Our panel of design experts evaluated hundreds of sites based on originality, navigation, product imagery, mobile responsiveness and product page design. Winners received cash prizes, a feature in Inc. Magazine and tickets to the Inc. 5000 Conference.

Marucci, a sports equipment and apparel brand, placed as best overall for its mastery of balancing high-quality, isolated product shots with vibrant colors and lifestyle imagery to lead customers through an exceptional purchase journey.

BEST IN APPAREL

NINE LINE APPAREL

Veteran-owned Nine Line Apparel won best design in the apparel category for its engaging homepage, detailed product pages and mobile-friendly responsive design, all of which contribute to a store that makes shopping easy and intuitive on any device.

BEST IN FOOD & BEVERAGE

DI BRUNO BROS.

Specialty food retailer Di Bruno Bros. was selected for its use of vivid product photography coupled with informative content and a user-friendly navigation designed to help customers explore and discover a wide range of culinary products.

BEST IN SPORTS

SISU GUARD

SISU Guard placed first in the sports and recreation category for bringing its brand to life through design, storytelling and an effective use of product reviews to compel customers to purchase its unique line of athletic mouthguards.

PEOPLE'S CHOICE

SIERRA DESIGNS

Sierra Designs received thousands of votes for its thoughtful navigation, crisp typography, large-scale product imagery and seamless customer experience.
