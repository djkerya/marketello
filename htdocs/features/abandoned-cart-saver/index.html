Automatically recover abandoned carts

Our built-in abandoned cart saver automatically sends a series of customizable emails to shoppers who leave during the checkout process, showing them what items they added and reminding them to come back and check out. Just turning on the abandoned cart saver helps recover 15% of lost sales on average, and has increased revenue by up to 50% for some merchants.

Customize your cart abandonment emails

Unlike other ecommerce solutions, BigCommerce gives you full control over when your abandoned cart emails send, how they look and what they say.

Timing

We’ve pre-set the frequency and send times based on best practices for increasing conversion with abandoned cart emails, but you can adjust to fit your needs.

Content

Deliver the most impactful messages for your shoppers by customizing your email subject lines and content.

Offers

You can automatically add a coupon code to your abandoned cart emails in order to increase conversion and recover lost sales.

Track your cart recovery

BigCommerce includes analytics that show you how much cart abandonment is costing your business — and how much revenue our abandoned cart saver is winning back. We make it easy to see all your abandoned carts and revenue for any date range, and track the number of recovered carts and total revenue recouped by our cart saver.
