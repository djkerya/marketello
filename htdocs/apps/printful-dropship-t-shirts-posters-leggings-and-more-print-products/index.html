Printful - Dropship T-Shirts, Posters, Leggings, and More Print Products

Client Reviews (27)

Automated drop shipping for t-shirts, posters, mugs and other print products

Your orders will be automatically sent from BigCommerce to us at Printful, to print and send out the order. Since everything is printed on-demand, you don't have to worry about keeping inventory, or guessing what sells best – we've got you covered.

How does Printful work?

When an order is made on your BigCommerce store that contains a Printful product, it is automatically sent directly to us, where we print and send your order to your customer. Printful also automatically updates tracking information for that item within BigCommerce.

Stuff you can get

  • T-shirts
  • Sweatshirts
  • Phone cases
  • Posters
  • Framed Posters
  • Canvas
  • Mugs
  • Totes
  • All-over t-shirts and tank tops
  • Cut & sew sublimation products: leggings, dresses, skirts
  • Socks
  • Pillows
  • Embroidery on hats and beanies
  • Socks

Extra perks

  • Sample orders are 20% off with free standard shipping worldwide
  • Create free mockup images and print files with our mockup generator
  • Add customized branding on all your orders (no Printful branding)
  • Use our design and ecommerce photography services to perfect your designs and photograph the finished results
  • Everything is printed with care at our fulfillment centers in California and North Carolina – we're one of the only dropshippers that doesn't outsource
So partner with an industry-leading dropshipper and start selling your products stress-free!

Pros: easy to set up, tons of products to choose from (shirts, totes, mugs, calendars, wall art, etc), fast

Cons: integration with Bigcommerce is a bit clunky (the way you associate an SKU with a Printful option is quite horrible), payment processing requires additional setup you will not find out about till you try to use it (customer pays you, you pay Printful - so you have to configure payments for Printful), the "wholesale" item cost is super high so this app is only of use to you if you cannot meet minimum quantities to get a batch of whatever printed out or to test an idea, shipping price is utterly ridiculous. Do not expect to make any sort of significant profit by selling these items. That said, it's still neat to be able to offer items with your logo and just because and not have to worry about  them beyond the initial setup.
