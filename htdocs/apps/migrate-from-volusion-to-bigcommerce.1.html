Migrate from Volusion to BigCommerce

Ready to switch ecommerce platforms?

Have you reached your growth potential on Volusion and want to take the next big step for your business? Migrate from Volusion to BigCommerce to unlock a bevy of powerful features and business tools to increase sales and simplify your online business.

How the migration works

Our dedicated Catalog Transfer Services team is the only one of its kind in the ecommerce space. We’ve helped move over 20,000 stores from other solutions, simplifying the migration process for your business. Our catalog transfers include the movement of product categories, options, and customers from your current store.

  • For complex or custom migrations, we can scope out the catalog transfer to account for the individual needs of your store
  • Facilitating the switch to BigCommerce includes the transfer of products, product images, product options, and more — see a full list of all the data we transfer
  • Transferring your data to BigCommerce doesn’t impact your existing store, so you don’t have to worry about missed sales.
  • Give your store the perfect look by choosing a high-converting ecommerce template designed for your vertical, or find a BigCommerce Certified Partner to recreate your current store’s design on our platform.
  • Don’t lose out on SEO: your catalog transfer includes 301 redirects for products and categories, which help Google and other search engines find pages on your new store
  • When you switch to BigCommerce, you’re in good hands: our comprehensive onboarding process makes it easy to use our platform. You also have access to 24/7 customer support on phone, chat, and email.

Want to hear a firsthand account of switching from Volusion to BigCommerce? Bombtech Golf made the move — and saw an immediate 300% increase in revenue. Read their story.

Why migrate from Volusion to BigCommerce?

  • Fair, predictable pricing with no transaction or bandwidth fees
  • Responsive website templates, built to convert mobile shoppers
  • Theme editor for making changes to your website, with no development required
  • Built-in integrations with Apple Pay, Amazon Pay, and more mobile wallets
  • Conversion tools such as abandoned cart saver, faceted search, and one-page checkout — none requiring extra plugins or apps
  • Multi-channel selling tools: BigCommerce is the only ecommerce platform to natively integrate with Facebook, Amazon, Pinterest, and eBay, giving you more ways to grow sales and improve your brand visibility
  • Special Braintree rates, starting at 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction and even lower (depending on BigCommerce plan). These rates are not available from Volusion, other ecommerce solutions, or directly from Braintree.
  • Best-in-class SEO: Your site is built for high organic search rankings, including optimized URLs, automated 301 redirects, and pre-optimized category and product pages
  • The best customer support in ecommerce: 24/7 phone, chat, and email. The BigCommerce Support team here is here to help you sell more around the clock.
  • Advance discount and promotion rules, making it easier for you to entice customers by offering coupons.
