Alibaba.com Wholesale

Client Reviews (15)
Source Products Reliably and Cost-Effectively

Alibaba.com, the leading platform for global wholesale trade, and Bigcommerce have made it possible for you to quickly and easily source unique products from the largest manufacturing hub in the world. We've brought together verified suppliers and low cost products just for Bigcommerce clients to reduce the complexity of sourcing products from China and to increase your confidence in cross-border trading.

Jointly developed by the two companies, the Alibaba.com Wholesale app is an integrated shopping experience for sourcing products from Alibaba.com's extensive network of Chinese wholesale suppliers right from the Bigcommerce control panel.

Benefits of using this app
  1. Integrated search, customized just for ecommerce merchants like you:This app provides a tailored search experience to quickly browse and purchase products in categories proven to sell well online
  2. No inventory requirements: You can simply place an order on Alibaba Wholesale after you receive a purchase order from your buyer. There is no minimum stocking or inventory requirements
  3. Customized pricing with lower order minimums: You have the option to select wholesale pricing based on fastest delivery time or lowest possible price
  4. Great variety of products: Search from a wide range of products via this app, across popular market categories including: apparel, automotive, beauty, consumer electronics and jewelry
  5. Selected suppliers*: We've identified the best performing suppliers on Alibaba Wholesale, verified them and ensure they sell at competitive prices for retailers using Bigcommerce
  6. Fast shipping and delivery: We partner with the world's top shipping providers (FedEx, UPS, DHL Express, EMS) to ensure that your products are delivered to you quickly
  7. Payment protection: Online merchants can source knowing that all orders placed via the Alibaba.com Wholesale app are backed by Alibaba.com's Secure Payment service.

*Terms and conditions of 'Selected Suppliers' apply (http://activities.alibaba.com/alibaba/wholesale-selected-supplier.php)

For more information about Alibaba Wholesale:http://activities.alibaba.com/alibaba/wholesale-guide.php

What The Point? (WTP) about 1 year ago by jas...com

1.  To create an app review, one much come back to the apps review page.  This could be improved by adding "create review" where someone that uses the app and enjoys it can easily provide their two cents.  It's inconvenient as-is.  I suspect people that dislike an app are probably more irritated by the time they figure this out.

2.   Positive Feedback: Alibaba is a great source for products, but it's a bit like searching the entire ocean for the perfect fish mate.  This app makes the Alibaba "ocean" a more manageable pond.  The items shown have high quality photos and clear product descriptions and terms.  All three of these things are far too uncommon on Alibaba.com.  

2.1  Oddly, a concerning number of sex toys appeared when I searched for "electronics tools."

3.  Suggested Improvements: Add functionality that doesn't exist on the proper Alibaba.com website.  This app is the digital equivalent of a "partners" flier that companies sometimes send in the mail.  The entire purpose and functionality of this app is to funnel Big Commerce users to the Alibaba.com website.

4.  Overall recommendation: 2 stars - If time were free, it still wouldn't be worth the time.  The second start is because the App is easy to use and easy to uninstall.  Skip this one and head to Alibaba.com.
