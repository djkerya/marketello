Better Coupon Box| Email Popups with Exit-Intent

Client Reviews (30)

Benefits:

  • Create beautiful email popups to convert visitors into email subscribers or social followers
  • Reward coupons to motivate new visitors to make their first purchase
  • Prevent cart abandonment with exit-intent popups

Features:

When customers first enter your store, a beautiful welcome popup will display, asking them to subscribe their email or follow your social media (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Pinterest, Google+) in return for a discount coupon.

The popup building process is easy without no coding skills required: just choose from our available options and your popup will be activated in 2 minutes.

Advanced features to customize and optimize conversion of your popups:

  • Create many popups running on different specific pages at the same time
  • Trigger popups to display to specific groups of customers only (new visitors or returning customers)
  • 100% Customizable popup templates with pre-built options or CSS custom to make them match with your store's theme design
  • Create exit-intent popups to prevent leaving customers and convert abandoned carts
