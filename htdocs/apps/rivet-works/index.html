Rivet Works

Client Reviews (1)

Rivet Works for BigCommerce allows customers to share their experiences with your products in photo, video, and testimonial formats. This SEO-rich, visual content is then automatically assigned to product SKUs and published to shoppable displays on product, category, and gallery pages. The result is a time-on-site increase of up to 275%, and conversion lifts of 244% for shoppers that view Rivet Works content.

Capture Content in the Formats Your Customers Share Most

For online shoppers, seeing is believing. Rivet Works' media upload capability allows your customers to submit multiple high-resolution photos and videos in a single submission.

Increase Engagement and Purchase Intent with Dynamic, Shoppable Displays

Rivet Works displays showcase a steady stream of visual content directly on site. This powerful content keeps shoppers engaged longer, guiding them to make a purchase.

Maximize Word-of-Mouth Marketing with One-Click Social Sharing

Customers can easily share experiences directly to their personal social pages. By clicking on a post, followers are directed back to your site to make a purchase of their own.

Easily Control What Ends up on Site — and What Doesn't

Rivet Works' moderation feature allows store owners to easily moderate content with a single click or tap.

Enhance Your Presence in Google Search Results

Rivet Works displays are optimized to be indexed by Google. Customer-generated content appears in Google search results in text, star rating, and visual formats.

Measure Your Progress with Conversion Tracking

Track conversion rates, average order value uplift, and engagement with Rivet's suite of robust analytics capabilities.
