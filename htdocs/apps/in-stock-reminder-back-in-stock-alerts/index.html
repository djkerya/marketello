In Stock Reminder - Back in Stock alerts

Client Reviews (15)

Are you periodically losing your clients, who would like to buy some products at your web store, but start to be disappointed and, as a result, leave your store, because they see "OUT OF STOCK" or "SOLD OUT" on a page of a desired product?

Or maybe you do not even suspect how many clients you are losing if there are no products in stock?

If you would like to change this situation and increase your revenue by recovering lost sales and encouraging future purchases,"In Stock Reminder" will help you with this!

How it works

  • "In Stock Reminder" lets your customers register an email address for an instant alert if they are interested in purchasing any product at yours web store that is currently out of stock. In this case, when the product becomes available for ordering, customers will receive an automatic notification about this in their email.
  • Small button will be displayed on pages of products that are sold out. When the button is clicked, a pop-up window will be opened, where a customer can leave their email.

Features for "Basic" plan of the app ($14.95/mo):

    • You can use a predefined button image or use your own button.
    • You can specify a position where you want to locate the button image on the screen.
    • You can set up the subscription pop-up window to open up automatically on the out of stock product pages.
    • Design of the pop-up window can be adjusted for any theme with a help of an excellent insightful editor with an online preview feature.
    • You can translate the registration form and email text into any language.
    • Rich statistics with graphics is available based on different data such as user emails, products with the most subscriptions, etc.
    • Possibility to check whether the out of stock product appears in stock every 4 hours and send e-mails automatically.

      Features for "Advanced" plan of the app ($24.95/mo):

      • All the features that are available for "Basic" plan.
      • Possibility to export all data to CSV format (e.g. for synchronization with MailChimp).
      • Possibility to add your own text or image as subscription badge into a required place on a product page.
      • Possibility to configure a particular in stock amount of some products that will trigger an email sent to customers only when the product in stock amount is equal to what has been configured.
      • Possibility to check whether the out of stock product appears in stock every 2 hours and sent send e-mails automatically.

      Features for "Unlimited" plan of the app ($34.95/mo):

      • All the feature that are available for "Basic" and "Advanced" plans.
      • Possibility to add subscription form directly into a required place on the product page.
      • Possibility to check whether the out of stock product appears in stock every 15 minutes and send e-mails automatically.
      You can try "In Stock Reminder" for free on your web store for 7 days.
