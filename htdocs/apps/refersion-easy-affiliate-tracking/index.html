Refersion: Easy Affiliate Tracking

Client Reviews (5)

Launch your Affiliate Program Today!

From the Developer of the Traffic Control app, Refersion is designed to help quickly launch your affiliate program. We handle the heavy lifting for you so that you can focus on building partnerships with bloggers, influencers, ambassadors, and so on. Zero technical knowledge required!

  • Support for shops of all sizes

We support stores of various sizes and backgrounds (and even currencies). Quite a few of our entrepreneurs have been featured on ABC's Shark Tank.

  • Get going with the Setup Guide

We built a Setup Guide to get you started. The guide helps you to connect your cart, add branding and adjust your account settings in less than 10 minutes.

  • Quickly register new affiliates

Fully customize your signup page with colors and content that reflect your branding. Share your program link on social media or embed it directly onto your website.

  • Never miss a beat with the dashboard

View affiliate performance rank with Top Performers, keep track of recent updates via Latest Activity feed and explore the location of top referral link visits with Clicks by Map.

  • Turn customers into affiliates, too

Invite new customers to join your program and earn rewards with the Post-Purchase Channel.

  • Affiliates can track individual stats

Affiliates are provided with their own dashboards, just like you. There they can access their unique link, update details, run reports and grab creatives to assist with marketing efforts.

  • Coupon Code Conversion Triggers

We offer 3 conversion trigger types to make tracking simple but coupon codes is an absolute fan favorite. Set up unique codes for affiliates to promote instead of links. Great for Instagram and Pinterest!

  • Full end-to-end tracking

We do all of the backend tracking for you. View things such as who drove the sale, customer and order details, and the site source. Plus we calculate the total commission due.

  • Easily pay affiliates with PayPal

Pay multiple affiliates at a time with PayPal Mass Payments. (See our FAQ below for info on alternative payout options).

  • Swift support from our US-based team

We are here to listen so if you get stuck or have some feedback to share, reach out to us anytime. Just click on the little chat badge to the right of your screen within the app to get in touch and our U.S. based support team will get back to you usually within 24-hours.

  • Gain new affiliates with the Refersion Marketplace

Join our marketplace and receive introductions to our large network of quality affiliates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What do you mean by affiliate sales?

Plans are based on monthly affiliate sales, which is the total amount of items purchased from your shop that are referred by your affiliates. This number does not include commissions paid out (there isn't a limit on payments).

Q. Is Refersion able to track more than one shopping cart/domain?

We can track multiple carts and domain sales within the same account. Get in touch with us for more info.

Q. Is PayPal required to pay my affiliates?

PayPal is not required to pay affiliates. You can opt to pay commissions with store credit, checks, money orders, etc.

Q. What type of commission structure do you support?

We offer the option to set a flat rate or a percentage of sale for your affiliates.

Q. Which currencies do you support?

We support a wide variety of currencies. If yours isn't listed, get in touch with us as we may be able to accommodate you.

Q. Do you offer plans with a lower percentage rate than Elite?

Absolutely! Reach out to us for further details. We have many happy customers using our Custom level plans.
